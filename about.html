The aim of this website is to educate students about online safety, highlight common cyber threats, and provide practical tips to prevent online attacks. By following the guidance presented here, students can reduce risks and enjoy a safer digital experience.

What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of defending computers, servers, mobile devices, electronic systems, networks, and data from malicious attacks. It combines technologies, processes, and practices to protect digital information from theft, damage, or unauthorized access.

Why Cybersecurity is Important

Students store personal information, academic records, and financial data online. Without proper cybersecurity awareness, they are at risk of identity theft, fraud, and exposure to harmful content. Learning cybersecurity early builds a strong foundation for safe online practices. Students also frequently use the internet for research, social media, online learning, and financial transactions. Without proper cybersecurity knowledge, they are exposed to cyber threats such as hacking, identity theft, and fraud.

Benefits of Cybersecurity Awareness

  1. Protects personal and academic information.
  2. Prevents financial loss due to online scams.
  3. Encourages responsible use of digital technology.
  4. Builds confidence in online activities.
